Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed and published by Riot Games. Officially released on June 2, 2020, the game has quickly become one of the most popular eSports titles in the world. Combining precise shooting mechanics with unique agent abilities, Valorant delivers a fresh take on the competitive shooter genre.

The game pits two teams of five players against each other in a round-based format. One team attacks by planting a spike (bomb), while the other defends. What sets Valorant apart is its cast of "agents," each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. This hybrid of CS:GO-style gunplay and hero-based mechanics gives the game strategic depth and a growing competitive scene.

Game Details

  • Developer: Riot Games
  • Release Date: June 2, 2020
  • Genre: Tactical FPS
  • Platform: PC (Windows)

Gameplay Roles

  • Duelists – Entry fraggers like Jett, Phoenix
  • Controllers – Smokes and map control (Omen, Viper)
  • Initiators – Break defenses (Sova, Skye)
  • Sentinels – Defense/Support (Sage, Killjoy)

eSports & Competitive Scene

  • VCT (Valorant Champions Tour)
  • Masters Tournaments
  • Challengers League
  • Valorant Champions – Year-end world championship
